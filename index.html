How do I connect Stripe to my Google Sheets?

To connect Stripe to Google Sheets, follow these steps:

  1. Open the "Stripe to Sheets" template in your AiVoiceHub dashboard
  2. Click "Connect Stripe" and authorize access to your Stripe account
  3. Select the Google Sheet you want to use (or create a new one)
  4. Map the Stripe fields to your sheet columns
  5. Set your sync frequency (real-time, daily, etc.)
